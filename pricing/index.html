Frequently Asked Questions

What is a snap board?
It is a board that has a limited lifetime. Typically 15 minutes. After this time the board becomes read only for another 24 hours while you can export its content. Then our system will delete it permanently.
Free is really free? Are there any hidden fees?
Yes, there is no payment required with the basic package, unless you would like to upgrade to Premium or Platinum.
What is a platinum badge?
It is a badge of honor for our platinum users that they can display as part of their avatar. If you log in to the application and go to the Profile page you can enable or disable it. What does the "Premium shapes for all" feature mean? This mean that every users editing a board created by a Platinum user can use premium shapes on the board regardless of their subscription status.
What does the "Premium shapes for all" feature mean?
This mean that every users editing a board created by a Platinum user can use premium shapes on the board regardless of their subscription status.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time.
How to change from yearly to monthly payments?
Log in to the application and go to the Subscription page and select the desired plan and billing cycle.
Where can I find my invoices?
All invoices will be emailed to customers at the beginning of the billing cycle and will also be available on the Billing page
Can I work offline with Riddey?
Unfortunately, collaborating without connection would be a tricky one, in order to have the best experience we would suggest you make sure you’re online.
Why can’t I log into my account?
To solve the problem, please try these steps:
  • Make sure you type your email correctly when you log in.
  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
  • If the problem persists, kindly contact our support team.
How can I restore deleted boards and content?
Users can access all deleted items in their trash and restore the board by either dragging out the board, or using the board action menu restore option.
