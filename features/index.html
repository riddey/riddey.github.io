Features

Discover our features

Canvas Operations

When using our platform, you'll have access to a wide range of basic canvas operations that make your creative journey effortless. You can express your ideas through drawing, correct or modify them with erasing, effortlessly select and transform elements, add text to convey your message effectively, and connect various components seamlessly using connectors.

These fundamental tools are designed to provide you with a versatile and intuitive canvas experience, ensuring that you can bring your vision to life with ease.

The Power of A.I

With our advanced AI drawing feature, you'll witness the magic of automation at its best. The system has the remarkable ability to instantly recognize hand-drawn shapes and effortlessly convert them into precise diagram entities, such as rectangles, triangles, ovals, and more.

This revolutionary capability not only saves you time and effort but also ensures that your diagrams and illustrations maintain a professional and polished appearance. Whether you're sketching out ideas, creating diagrams, or illustrating concepts, our AI-powered drawing tool will transform your freehand creations into accurate, refined shapes, elevating the quality and efficiency of your work.

Igniting Creativity

Our platform offers a unique blend of features designed to enhance your creative experience. With a limited color palette consisting of only six colors, you can focus on the essence of your work without overwhelming choices. Additionally, our snap boards, featuring time-constrained canvases, are specifically crafted to ignite your creativity by encouraging quick thinking and spontaneous ideation.

These combined features provide you with a refreshing and efficient way to channel your creativity and bring your ideas to life with clarity and innovation.

Elevate your Collaboration

Our platform offers convenient export options, allowing users to export board content in either raster image formats like PNG or JPG or as vector images in SVG format. Whether you need high-resolution images for print or scalable vector graphics for digital use, we've got you covered. Additionally, our real-time multiplayer functionality creates a collaborative environment where everyone can view and interact with the board content simultaneously.

This feature fosters seamless communication and enhances teamwork as users can observe and participate in real-time, making collaboration a breeze.
