Riddey.Set.Go - Introducing Riddey, your online canvas of Innovation!

With technology at the forefront of all work functionality, effective collaboration is more crucial than ever. That’s why we’re excited to introduce Riddey, your new go-to online virtual whiteboard. With Riddey, you can empower your teams to collaborate from anywhere in the world, foster creativity, and streamline your creative processes, all while enjoying an array of innovative features designed to make your work easier and more productive.

Unleash Your Creativity with Riddey’s Versatile Boards

At Riddey, we understand that every project and brainstorming session is unique. That’s why we offer a range of board options to cater to your specific needs:

Permanent Whiteboards

For projects that require ongoing development and reference, our permanent white and black boards provide a dependable space for your ideas and concepts.

Snap Boards

Need a quick and temporary canvas for those spur-of-the-moment creative bursts? Riddey’s snap boards are designed just for that. The lifespan ranges from 15 minutes to 2 hours, encouraging you to be creative in short, focused sessions.

AI-Powered Shape Transformation for Effortless Diagrams

Riddey doesn’t just stop at basic drawing tools. Our platform boasts cutting-edge AI technology that can transform your hand-drawn shapes into precise diagrams effortlessly. Whether you’re sketching out a concept, illustrating an idea, or presenting a diagram, Riddey’s AI ensures your creations are polished and professional.

Effortless Export and No Tool UI for Seamless Workflow

Exporting your board content is a breeze with Riddey. Whether you need a high-resolution raster image (PNG or JPG) or a scalable vector image (SVG), Riddey has you covered. Share your creations effortlessly with colleagues and clients.

But that’s not all – we take user experience to the next level with our “No Tool UI” approach. Riddey allows you to easily transition between interaction modes without the need to constantly switch back and forth between toolboxes. This seamless workflow ensures that your creative process remains uninterrupted, saving you time and frustration.

Collaborate from Anywhere, Anytime

With Riddey, geographical boundaries are a thing of the past. Our platform allows teams to collaborate from anywhere in the world. Whether you’re in the same office or on opposite sides of the globe, Riddey brings you together on a virtual canvas, promoting effective communication and productive teamwork.

Riddey is more than just an online whiteboard; it’s a powerful tool designed to enhance your creativity, productivity, and collaboration. Join us on this exciting journey as we give you the perfect canvas to share ideas, and bring your visions to life.
